Shree premanand ji maharaj

premanand ji maharaj is the brahmachari of vrindavan , uttar pradesh . a member of a brahmin family in uttar pradesh ,he left his family at age of 13 to become a monk and lived in an ashram in vrindavan

Biography

Premananda Ji Maharaj was born in 1963 in a poor Brahmin family in Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, India. His childhood name was Aniruddha Kumar Pandey, his father's name was Sri Shambhu Pandey, and his mother's name was Smt. Rama Devi. His grandfather was a monk. His family atmosphere was very holy, pure and devotional. Saints and Mahatmas used to come and go to his grandfather's house to become a monk. Aniruddha Kumar Pandey i.e. Premananda Ji used to listen very attentively to the spiritual discourses of Saints and Mahatmas during his childhood. As a result he became very attracted to spirituality, In 1976, when he was only 13 years old, he left his family in the dark of night and crossed the road to Vrindavan. He came to Vrindavan and worshiped the Lord and begged for food, people knew him as Brahmacharya as Ananda Swarap.


Education :-


He was a son of a Hindu Brahmin family and his grandfather was a monk so from childhood he engaged himself in sadhu seva and other services. At the age of ten he completed the study of all the verses of the Bhagavad Gita, when he was in class nine he vowed to renounce all to live a spiritual life in search of God. At the age of 13, he left his family one night and came to Vrindavan in search of the Supreme Soul and started his sadhana from there. At present he is in the ashram of Vrindavan.
